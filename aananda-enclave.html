Welcome to Aananda Enclave: A Sanctuary of Serenity and Elegance

Our group has prominently been in Textiles for the past six decades. Over the years, we have built over 3,00,000 sq. ft of real estate ranging from various factories to an iconic building, the "Surya Towers'' located in the heart of Secunderabad.

With our rich experience in the real estate sector we are ready to launch our latest premium offering, Aananda Enclave, a plotting venture on the Hyderabad-Warangal NH 163 in Bhongir town, Telangana.

Location Highlights


The telangana government is heavily promotiving investments in n Eastern part of Hyderabad. Aananda Enclave is nestled in the middle of a strategic growth opportunity. On one side, falls AIIMS, the proposed IT parks of Bibinagar and Ghatkesar, the ORR and the upcoming area of Uppal, whereas on the other side falls the the newly renovated temple of Yadagirigutta and district headquarters, Bhongir town.

  • 20 mins Drive to ORR
  • 10 mins Drive to AIIMS
  • 10 mins Drive to Bhongir Railway Station
  • 7 mins Drive to Bhongir Bus Stand
  • 60 mins Drive to RGIA
  • 45 mins Drive to Uppal X Roads
