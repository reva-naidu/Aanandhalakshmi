Heritage

A part of the Suryavanshi group established in the 1960’s, our legacy is built upon decades of unparalleled commitment to quality and innovation. With deep-rooted expertise originating from spinning mills and advancing to real estate, our journey has been marked by tested milestones. In the 1980s, the Suryavanshi Group etched its name in history by erecting the iconic Surya Towers, a testament to our pioneering spirit, in the heart of Secunderabad, becoming the tallest edifice in the city at the time.

Today, Aanandalakshmi is propelled by a renewed vision, channelling our resources towards the burgeoning real estate sector and crafting specialized textile products. Guided by a relentless pursuit of growth, sustainable innovation, and a customer-centric ethos, we stand poised to redefine industry standards and shape the future of our communities.
