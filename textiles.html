Aanandalakshmi Cotton Linters: Premium Quality for Superior Products

We are a leading Indian Processors for high grade Cotton Linters for clients Worldwide.

Strategically based out in the geographical centre of India, we have a distinct advantage logistically in sourcing high quality cotton seeds from the Cotton producing states in India.

Our Moto: Quality Over Quantity


We are passionately involved in cleaning of Cotton Linter and meeting the stringent requirements of our clients globally.

We successfully separate and remove fine dust, mud, gravel, seed coat fragments, leaf particles, stems, dirty fibres and other foreign particles through our In-House cleaning infrastructure.

Our Process and More


We employ the processes of Pneumatic cleaning, Mechanical cylinder beater with Screen Moting, Perforated screen shakers with aspiration, Air Separators with filter cloth, Fiber compactor with prime and secondary filters. With these processes we ensure to meet the required final cotton linter parameters. Consistently.

  • We are capable to supply 200 MT - 600 MT per month.
  • Lower Contamination and Impurities in Cotton Linters with increased cellulose content.
  • Consistent Quality.
  • Customised and Flexible deliveries.
